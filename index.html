Joseon Dynasty

The ancient Joseon dynasty was the last dynasty of Korea and the longest-standing Confucian dynasty, founded by Yi Seong-gye. A legacy and a culture still found in modern Korea.

The name of the last emperator in Joseon era was Yunghui

Ruling from 1907 to 1910. He was the fourth son of Emperor Gojong. Called Crown Prince Yi Cheok, he was named Yung-hui Emperor when the Japanese forced the abdication of his father, and his tenure ended with the annexation of Korea by Japan in 1910.
